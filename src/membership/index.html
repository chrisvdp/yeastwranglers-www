Membership Description Cost Annual member Annual memberships are good for the calendar year and allow you to attend all club meetings without paying a drop-in fee. Annual members will also have their shipping charges subsidized if the member submits entries in a club-sanctioned brewing competition. Annual members may also receive discounts at selected merchants. A family membership discount is also available at $50/year. Click here to apply for an annual membership. An option for online membership purchase is available once a membership form has been completed online. $30/year Drop-in member Drop-in memberships are also available to attend individual club meetings. The cost per meeting is $10 which subsidizes the cost of commercial beer samples. Annual members are not required to pay drop-in fees. $10/meeting
