Memberships are good for the calendar year and allow you to attend all club meetings without paying a drop-in fee. Annual members will also have their shipping charges subsidized if the member submits entries in a club-sanctioned brewing competition. Annual members may also receive discounts at selected merchants.

Drop-in memberships are also available to attend individual club meetings. Your first meeting is free, then each drop-in afterwards is $10. Annual members are not required to pay drop-in fees.
